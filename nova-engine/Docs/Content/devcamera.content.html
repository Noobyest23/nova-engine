FreeCam (Developer Camera)

Inherits: Camera2D

Description

The FreeCam is a built-in debugging tool available in Debug Builds. It allows you to decouple the view from the game's logic to inspect the scene, check clipping, or find hidden objects.

At the start of a scene, the engine automatically injects the Developer Camera at root/DevCamera. You can toggle between your game's active camera and the FreeCam by pressing F1.

Hierarchy Note: The engine searches the scene tree from top to bottom. If you move a game camera above DevCamera in the hierarchy and mark it as active, the toggle may fail to override it.

Controls

Input Action
W A S D Move Camera
Mouse Wheel Zoom In / Out
Shift (Hold) Turbo Move (Increases speed)
F1 Toggle FreeCam On/Off
F2 Open Console (Pause Game)

Internal Behavior

When activated, the FreeCam sets its active property to true and disables the active property on the previously used camera. Upon deactivation, it restores the original camera state.
